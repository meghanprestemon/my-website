welcome.

I am an aspiring software engineer. I have experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and some Bootstrap. I am currently completing the Free Code Camp online course and will be starting the in-person Galvanize bootcamp in April.

aboutMe.

I am originally from upstate New York - near Syracuse which is literally the snowiest city in the United States; beating the next snowiest city by over 25 inches. I now live in Alameda with my husband, Rob. We've been in the Bay Area for about 4 years and are happy to call it home.

Before starting my journey into web development I had quite a few different jobs in varying fields. I got my bachelors degree in psychology and was a counselor when I lived in New York. After leaving New York I've had positions as an administrative assistant for different organizations including a law firm, a state university, and a mental health nonprofit. I have also been the manager of an organic mattress retailer and manager of a yoga studio.

Finding a lack of fulfillment and engagement in these fields I knew it was time for something new and challenging... so here I am!
